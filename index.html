ABOUT

About us

LogX Networks is founded to create a unique approach in the networking industry to connect freight forwarders, and create an ecosystem of network providing both digital and network solutions.

We put our eXtreme care in connecting freight forwarders and providing them best solutions and an environment built on connections and relationships. Our goal is to provide networking solutions for the freight forwarders to connect and explore opportunities to help them grow financially.




Logistics industry is dominated by multinationals, and we provide our eXpertise to connect the freight forwarders to compete with the multinationals, and improve their operations by connecting with their global partners.

We are present globally, and can connect with you the members worldwide to provide the best solutions. We are here to serve and protect our members, with our team.
