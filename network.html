OUR NETWORKS

Our Networks

Network is composed of accomplished cargo specialists, adept at managing diverse cargo types adhering to stringent professional standards. Our comprehensive solutions ensure the secure and seamless transportation of your customers’ cargo from point A to B. At LogX Global, our mission is to foster a sense of community within the Freight Forwarding Industry, empowering you to expand your business horizons through strategic global partnerships.

Network of Time-Critical experts handling your shipments in a diverse and customer-oriented industry that requires fast response and service. A network of true professionals that are able to provide you with 24/7 service, Air on Ground, onboard couriers, Next Flight out solutions, and warehousing at any airport in the world. With strong Airlines contracts and exclusive per airport membership your cargo will be in good hands

A premier, specialized Projects Logistics network of independent project logistics forwarders, we cater to professional projects logistics encompassing global movements through Air, Sea, and Land, inclusive of intricate maneuvers involving heavy lifts and the precise handling of oversized, out-of-gauge, and weighty cargo. The intricacies of managing project Cargo demand a high level of professionalism and expertise, an area in which our members excel with their robust knowledge and skill set.

LogX Cold Chain is an eminent global network specializing in temperature-sensitive logistics, comprising professionals well-versed in the intricacies of transporting pharmaceuticals and perishable goods. Within our network, our members enjoy access to cutting-edge equipment essential for ensuring secure and efficient transit from point of origin to final delivery, along with access to cold storage facilities vital for the preservation and seamless flow of products throughout the supply chain.

ABOUT

LogX Networks was founded to create a unique approach in the networking industry to connect freight forwarders and create an ecosystem of networks providing both digital and network solutions.
We put our eXtreme care into connecting freight forwarders and providing them best solutions and an environment built on connections and relationships. Our goal is to provide networking solutions for the freight forwarders to connect and explore opportunities to help them grow financially.
The logistics industry is dominated by multinationals, and we provide our eXpertise to connect the freight forwarders to compete with the multinationals and improve their operations by connecting with their global partners.
We are present globally and can connect with you the members worldwide to provide the best solutions. We are here to serve and protect our members, with our team.
Welcome to LogX Networks...
We invite you to explore our network group and discover how Log X Networks can transform your approach to global trade and logistics. Whether you are a business looking for a logistics partner or an individual seeking reliable shipping solutions, we meet your needs.
